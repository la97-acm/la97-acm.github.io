Daybreak Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Overview

Daybreak is a local-first alarm scheduling app for iPhone and iPad. By default, your alarm groups, schedules, overrides, and alarm settings stay on your device.

What Daybreak stores on your device

  • Alarm group names, colors, times, and alarm settings
  • Weekly schedules, day-specific overrides, and loose alarms
  • Schedule pauses and local onboarding or refresh preferences

What Daybreak does not collect

  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No cross-app tracking
  • Daybreak does not currently use accounts or cloud sync

When data can leave your device

  • When you export a backup
  • When you import a backup or group template
  • When you share a group template through Mail, Messages, Files, or another service
  • When you subscribe, Apple processes the purchase through StoreKit

Permissions

  • Alarm access to schedule alarms through AlarmKit
  • File access through Apple system pickers and share sheets when you import or export files
